Hi! Welcome to my website. I'm a PhD Candidate at the [Paris School of Economics](https://www.parisschoolofeconomics.eu/) and Collège de France at the [Farhi Innovation Lab](https://www.farhi-innovation-lab.fr/), under the supervision of [Philippe Aghion](https://www.philippeaghion.com/) in applied microeconomics. I work on research and public  organisational reforms in France, as well as the impact of political pressures on research. **I will be on the 2026-2027 Job Market.**


Seminar
========

I have been co-organising since 2023 the Brown Bag Seminar of Innovation, a seminar of the Farhi Lab and the economics department at Insead. It is held once a month, both online and physically at the Collège de France, 3 rue d'Ulm, on Friday mornings. You will find all of the information about upcoming sessions on the [lab's calendar](https://www.farhi-innovation-lab.fr/events/) or [our page on the PSE website](https://www.parisschoolofeconomics.eu/evenements/brown-bag-economics-of-innovation-seminar/).
