About Raphaël Wargon

Welcome to my website! I am a PhD candidate at the Paris School of Economics and Collège de France, working at the Farhi Innovation Lab, under the supervision of Philippe Aghion. My research focuses on applied microeconomics, specifically on the labor market for researchers in France, as well as the influence of political pressures on research.

Research Interests: Economics of innovation, political economy, labor economics, bibliometrics.

Seminar Information: The Farhi Innovation lab's PhD students co-organise the Brown Bag Seminar of Innovation with Insead once a month. It is held both online and physically at the Collège de France, 3 rue d'Ulm, on Friday mornings. You will find all of the information about upcoming sessions on the PSE website. Feel free to contact Luc Paluskiewicz, Milla Tarpola or me if you have any questions on the seminar or want to present.
